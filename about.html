About DEV

Powering Builders on Stellar

What is DEV?

DEV is a next-generation digital asset on the Stellar blockchain, designed specifically for developers, innovators, and protocol creators. It offers ultra-fast, low-cost transactions and empowers global decentralized applications to scale securely.

Why Stellar?

We chose Stellar for its speed, reliability, and decentralization. With built-in smart contract primitives, trustlines, and liquidity pools, Stellar is the perfect ecosystem for DEV to thrive in.
